What's a Link?

A Link is a single booking with a Momni — and it's $1, every time you book. Each booking is one Link. That dollar is the only thing Momni ever charges; the care itself you pay your Momni directly, and they keep every penny.

Momni is a community platform, not a childcare provider. Members make their own care decisions and pay each other directly.
